# Track Brewing Progress

Monitor your long-term improvement and get personalized practice plans.

## Overview

This playbook analyzes your brewing history to identify trends, generate practice prescriptions, and keep you motivated with weekly summaries.

## Features

### Trend Analysis
- Score progression over time
- Stability improvement tracking
- Identification of weak/strong segments
- Practice streak counting

### Practice Prescription
Based on your data, you receive:
- Focus segment to prioritize
- Specific drill with instructions
- Realistic improvement timeline

### Weekly Summary
- Sessions completed
- Average and best scores
- Encouragement message
- Goal for next week

## Sample Drills

- **Metronome Pour**: Use 60 BPM beat to develop consistent rhythm
- **Gentle Bloom**: Practice slow, even saturation during bloom
- **Finish Strong**: Maintain focus through final pour

## Growth Visualization

View your score progression as a chart to see improvement over days and weeks.

## Privacy

All progress data is stored locally. Your brewing journey stays yours.
