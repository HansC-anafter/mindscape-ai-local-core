# Generate Coach Feedback

Get friendly, encouraging coaching advice from your AI brewing buddy.

## Overview

This playbook generates personalized coaching feedback based on your session analysis. The feedback is designed to be encouraging, specific, and actionable.

## Coaching Style

Your coach:
- Talks like a supportive friend, not a strict instructor
- Celebrates your effort, even when results aren't perfect
- Focuses on ONE improvement at a time
- Gives concrete, actionable tips

## What You Get

### Friendly Recap
A warm, 20-40 character summary of your session.

### Observations (Max 2)
Specific observations referencing actual data from your session.

### Micro Task
ONE focused task for your next brewing session.

### Tracking Metric
A specific metric to track your improvement over time.

## Coaching Modes

- **Quiet**: Minimal prompts, best for experienced users
- **Coach**: Balanced guidance (default)
- **Strict**: More detailed feedback for intensive practice

## What the Coach WON'T Do

- Predict flavor outcomes
- Make cupping judgments
- Overwhelm you with multiple suggestions
- Use condescending language
